Show VRML Model


The java-applet shows the model. You are able to rotate the displayed
object simply with dragging the mouse into the desired direction.
You can also zoom in and out by holding down the ctrl-key and dragging
the mouse back and forth. The cursor keys can also be used to rotate/zoom the object
